About Our Confirmation Program

The Sacrament of Confirmation is a significant milestone in the Catholic faith journey. At St. Anthony de Padua Parish, we guide young Catholics through comprehensive spiritual preparation, helping them to embrace the Holy Spirit and strengthen their commitment to Christ and the Church.

Our program focuses on:

  • Deepening personal relationship with Jesus Christ
  • Understanding Catholic teachings and traditions
  • Building a supportive faith community
  • Encouraging active participation in parish life
  • Service to others as disciples of Christ

Volunteer Hours Guidelines

Please read and understand these rules before logging volunteer hours.
General Rules
  1. Be honest and accurate when reporting your volunteer hours.
  2. Log your hours as soon as possible after completing volunteer work.
  3. Round to the nearest half hour (e.g., 2.5 hours, not 2.33).
  4. Include a brief description of the work you performed.
  5. Contact the volunteer coordinator if you have any questions.
Eligible Volunteer Activities
  • Church ministry support (altar serving, choir, etc.)
  • Parish events and fundraisers
  • Community outreach programs
  • Assisting with religious education
  • Other pre-approved volunteer activities
Requirements

Confirmation candidates must complete a minimum of 20 service hours during the program year.

Hours should be distributed across different types of service to ensure a well-rounded experience.

Any attempt to falsify volunteer hours may result in disciplinary action.
